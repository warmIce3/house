# Ongoing Health Trends regarding Depression and in the Virgin Islands

### 1. Summary: This is a capstone project that analyzes a public health dataset.

### 2. The problem, those who are affected: The people who are affected are all of those with depression, diabetes, and those who smoke, as well as those who live in the Virgin Islands.

### 3. Data: The data set was originally cleaned, fixing duplicates and missing values. Trends regarding the Virgin Islands and Depression were found using Machine Models and Charts.

### 4. Findings: It was found that those in the Virgin Islands are much more likely to possess diabetes, smoke, and be depressed.
